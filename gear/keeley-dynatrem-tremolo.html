Keeley Dynatrem Tremolo





The Keeley Dynatrem Tremolo is a highly versatile tremolo pedal designed to recreate classic amplifier tremolo effects while adding modern control options. The pedal offers multiple waveform modes including harmonic tremolo, sine wave modulation, and dynamic tremolo that responds to playing intensity. This allows guitarists to achieve subtle vintage pulse, rhythmic chop, or expressive modulation that reacts to performance dynamics. The intuitive controls make it easy to adjust speed, depth, and waveform characteristics to match different musical styles. From classic surf tremolo to modern ambient textures, the Dynatrem delivers rich modulation with smooth transitions. Built with Keeley’s high-quality engineering, the pedal provides reliable performance and flexible tremolo tones for both studio and stage use.
