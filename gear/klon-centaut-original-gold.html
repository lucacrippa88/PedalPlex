Klon Centaut Original - Gold





The Klon Centaur Original Gold is one of the most iconic boutique overdrive pedals ever built. Famous for its transparent tone and exceptional touch sensitivity, the Centaur enhances the natural sound of a guitar and amplifier without masking their character. Its unique circuitry blends a clean signal with an overdriven path, allowing the pedal to maintain clarity and articulation even at higher gain settings. Guitarists often use the Centaur as a powerful clean boost to push tube amplifiers or as a smooth, harmonically rich overdrive. The pedal features straightforward Gain, Treble, and Output controls that provide a wide tonal range suitable for many musical styles. The gold enclosure with the distinctive Centaur artwork has become a symbol of boutique pedal design. The Klon Centaur Original Gold remains one of the most sought-after overdrive pedals in guitar history.
