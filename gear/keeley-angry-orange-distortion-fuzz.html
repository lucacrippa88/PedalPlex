Keeley Angry Orange Distortion & Fuzz





The Keeley Angry Orange Distortion & Fuzz combines two powerful gain circuits inspired by classic British amp distortion and vintage fuzz tones. Designed in collaboration with guitarists seeking aggressive yet musical drive, the pedal offers a wide range of saturation from crunchy distortion to thick, sustaining fuzz. Its responsive circuitry maintains strong note articulation even at higher gain levels, making it suitable for heavy rhythm playing and expressive lead tones. The control layout allows players to shape gain intensity, tonal balance, and output level with precision. The Angry Orange excels when pushing tube amplifiers or stacking with other pedals for layered gain textures. With its bold character and versatile sound shaping, this pedal captures the spirit of classic rock and modern alternative guitar tones while maintaining the reliability and craftsmanship associated with Keeley Electronics.
