Anasounds Ages Harmonic Tremolo





The Anasounds Ages Harmonic Tremolo recreates the complex harmonic tremolo effect found in vintage American amplifiers. Unlike standard tremolo circuits that simply modulate volume, harmonic tremolo splits the signal into different frequency bands and modulates them independently, creating a swirling and dimensional effect. The Ages produces warm vintage modulation that works beautifully for blues, surf, indie, and psychedelic guitar styles. Discover classic harmonic tremolo textures with the Anasounds Ages on PedalPlex.
