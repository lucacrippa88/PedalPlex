Joyfx Custom Pedals LoveTones - Superbolt + Lightspeed Dual Overdrive





The Joyfx Custom Pedals LoveTones Superbolt + Lightspeed Dual Overdrive combines two boutique-inspired overdrive circuits into one powerful pedal. The Superbolt-style side provides amp-like overdrive with rich harmonic content, while the Lightspeed-style circuit focuses on transparent low-gain drive and dynamic response. Each side can be used independently or stacked to create layered overdrive textures with enhanced sustain and complexity. The pedal offers flexibility for switching between rhythm and lead tones without needing multiple pedals. Both circuits maintain clarity and respond well to guitar volume adjustments. Built with attention to tone and durability, the Joyfx LoveTones Superbolt + Lightspeed Dual Overdrive is an excellent option for players seeking versatile boutique-style drive combinations.
