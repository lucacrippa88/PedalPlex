Description

The 1981 Inventions DRV is a modern distortion pedal inspired by the classic ProCo RAT circuit, refined for clarity, balance, and studio-grade tone. Designed by Matthew Hoopes, the DRV delivers a wide gain range that moves from gritty overdrive to saturated distortion while maintaining exceptional note definition. The simple three-knob layout (Volume, Tone, Drive) makes it easy to dial in tight rhythm tones, articulate lead sounds, or thick, punchy distortion for modern pedalboards. What sets the DRV apart is its carefully tuned EQ and dynamic response, allowing it to cut through a mix without sounding harsh or muddy. Guitarists appreciate its ability to stack well with other pedals and its consistent performance across different amps. Built with premium components and housed in a durable enclosure, the DRV has become a favorite among boutique pedal enthusiasts and professional players alike.
