Keeley Fuzz Head - Blue





The Keeley Fuzz Head Blue edition delivers the same hybrid germanium fuzz circuit as the original model while featuring a distinctive blue enclosure. Known for its smooth and dynamic response, the pedal blends vintage fuzz warmth with modern clarity and articulation. The circuit reacts naturally to guitar volume changes, allowing players to move between clean tones, overdrive, and full fuzz saturation. This versatility makes it suitable for a wide range of musical styles including blues, classic rock, and indie. With its expressive tone and reliable construction, the Fuzz Head Blue edition offers classic fuzz character in a visually striking design.
