A3 Stompbox Groovim Distortion





The A3 Stompbox Groovim Distortion delivers rich saturation and balanced tonal response for modern guitar rigs. Designed to produce tight rhythm tones and expressive lead sounds, the pedal offers strong output and clear articulation across a wide gain range. Carefully tuned EQ ensures that the distortion remains defined without excessive harshness or muddy low end. The Groovim Distortion works well both as a standalone drive pedal and as part of a stacked gain setup with boosts or overdrives.
