29 Pedals Toki





The 29 Pedals TOKI is a versatile overdrive pedal designed to deliver responsive gain and detailed harmonic character. Built with boutique craftsmanship and premium components, the TOKI provides a wide range of tones from light breakup to rich, expressive overdrive. The circuit is engineered for strong dynamic response, allowing the pedal to react naturally to picking attack and guitar volume adjustments. Its balanced EQ ensures clarity across the frequency spectrum, making it suitable for both rhythm and lead applications. Guitarists often use the TOKI as a primary drive or as part of a stacked gain setup with boosts and distortion pedals.
