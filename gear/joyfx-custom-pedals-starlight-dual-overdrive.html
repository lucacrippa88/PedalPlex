Joyfx Custom Pedals Starlight Dual Overdrive





The Joyfx Custom Pedals Starlight Dual Overdrive is designed to provide two complementary overdrive circuits within a single pedal. Each channel can be used independently or stacked to produce layered gain textures with enhanced sustain and harmonic complexity. The pedal allows players to quickly switch between different gain levels for rhythm and lead parts during performance. Both circuits emphasize clarity and dynamic response, ensuring chords remain articulate even with increased saturation. The Starlight Dual Overdrive works well with a wide variety of guitars and amplifiers and can easily integrate into complex pedalboard setups. Built with the quality craftsmanship associated with Joyfx Custom Pedals, this unit offers flexible boutique-style overdrive tones.
