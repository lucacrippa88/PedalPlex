JHS Notaklön





The JHS Notaklön is a tongue-in-cheek tribute to one of the most famous transparent overdrive circuits ever created. Designed with the same spirit of clarity and dynamic response, it provides a clean boost and light overdrive that enhances the natural tone of the guitar and amplifier. The pedal emphasizes headroom, articulation, and a subtle midrange presence that helps guitars sit well in a mix. It works particularly well as a boost pushing tube amplifiers or stacking with other drive pedals. Despite its playful name, the Notaklön offers professional-level tone shaping for modern pedalboards.
