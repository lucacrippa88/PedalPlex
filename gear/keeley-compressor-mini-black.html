Keeley Compressor Mini - Black





The Keeley Compressor Mini Black edition delivers studio-quality compression in an extremely compact pedal format. Designed for guitarists who want smooth dynamic control without sacrificing pedalboard space, the pedal offers a simple two-knob layout for compression and level adjustment. Despite its minimal design, the circuit provides the signature Keeley compression known for adding sustain, clarity, and balanced dynamics. The pedal works equally well for clean rhythm tones, country picking, funk grooves, and sustaining lead lines. Its transparent response preserves the natural character of the guitar while smoothing out volume peaks. Built with high-quality components and reliable construction, the Keeley Compressor Mini Black is a powerful yet space-efficient solution for dynamic tone shaping.
