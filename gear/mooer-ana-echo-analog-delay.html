MOOER Ana Echo Analog Delay





The Mooer Ana Echo Analog Delay is a compact pedal designed to recreate the warm, natural repeats of classic analog delay units. Using bucket-brigade style circuitry, the pedal produces soft and musical echoes that gradually degrade over time, creating a vintage character favored by many guitarists. Controls for delay time, feedback, and level allow players to dial in everything from subtle slapback to atmospheric ambient repeats. The small enclosure makes the Ana Echo ideal for space-conscious pedalboards while still delivering rich analog tone. True bypass switching ensures signal integrity when the effect is disengaged. The Mooer Ana Echo is a popular choice for players seeking classic analog delay warmth in a miniature format.
