Joyfx Custom Pedals 1981 Mini Distortion





The Joyfx Custom Pedals 1981 Mini Distortion captures the punchy and articulate character inspired by classic boutique distortion circuits in a compact format. Designed for players who want tight low end, clear mids, and responsive gain, this pedal excels at modern rock, alternative, and dynamic lead tones. The control layout is simple yet flexible, typically including gain, tone, and volume adjustments that allow the pedal to move from edge-of-breakup grit to saturated distortion. Despite its small footprint, the 1981 Mini Distortion retains excellent note separation and touch sensitivity, making it suitable for stacking with other drives or pushing a tube amp. Joyfx focuses on handmade craftsmanship and reliable components, ensuring consistent performance both in studio and on stage. For players seeking boutique-style distortion tones in a pedalboard-friendly enclosure, the Joyfx 1981 Mini Distortion delivers clarity, punch, and modern drive character.
