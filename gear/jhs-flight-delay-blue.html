JHS Flight Delay Blue





The JHS Flight Delay Blue is a compact digital delay designed for clear, musical echo effects with intuitive controls. It offers a range of delay times suitable for slapback, rhythmic repeats, and ambient textures. The pedal maintains excellent signal clarity even with higher feedback settings. Its streamlined design makes it ideal for pedalboards where space is limited while still delivering professional delay sounds.
