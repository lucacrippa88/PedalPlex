Joyfx Custom Pedals Footswitch Aux Hx Stomp / PodGO





The Joyfx Custom Pedals Footswitch Aux for HX Stomp and POD Go is a practical accessory designed to expand the control capabilities of popular multi-effects processors. By adding external footswitch functionality, this device allows players to trigger additional presets, effects, or tap tempo commands without navigating menus during performance. The footswitch integrates easily with compatible devices such as the HX Stomp and POD Go through the auxiliary control input. Its rugged construction ensures reliable operation on stage, while the compact footprint keeps pedalboards organized. Musicians who rely on multi-effects processors often need additional real-time control, and this auxiliary footswitch provides a simple solution. Whether switching snapshots, toggling effects blocks, or controlling tap tempo, the Joyfx Aux Footswitch enhances workflow and performance flexibility for modern digital rigs.
