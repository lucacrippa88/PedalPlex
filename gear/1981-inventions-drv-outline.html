1981 Inventions DRV (Outline)





The 1981 Inventions DRV (Outline) is a boutique distortion pedal celebrated for its articulate tone and refined take on the classic RAT-style circuit. Designed to provide a wide gain range with exceptional clarity, the DRV can move from tight overdrive textures to powerful distortion while maintaining definition and balance. Its three controls—Volume, Tone, and Drive—allow players to quickly dial in focused rhythm tones, cutting leads, or thick distortion suitable for modern pedalboards. One of the defining qualities of the DRV is its smooth clipping and carefully tuned EQ, which prevents harsh highs and muddy lows. This makes it highly effective both as a standalone distortion and as part of a stacked gain setup. The Outline finish gives the pedal a clean and distinctive aesthetic while delivering the same acclaimed performance.
