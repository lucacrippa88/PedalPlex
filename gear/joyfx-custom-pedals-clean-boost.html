Joyfx Custom Pedals Clean Boost





The Joyfx Custom Pedals Clean Boost is a straightforward yet powerful pedal designed to increase signal level without altering the core tone of your guitar. Ideal for pushing tube amplifiers into natural breakup or elevating lead parts in a mix, this pedal provides transparent gain with minimal coloration. The simple control layout allows players to quickly dial in the desired boost level, making it perfect for live performance situations where reliability and ease of use are essential. When placed before drive pedals, the Clean Boost can add saturation and sustain, while positioning it after overdrives increases overall volume for solos. Joyfx’s build quality ensures low noise and consistent performance across different rigs. Whether used to push an amp, balance levels between guitars, or add presence to lead lines, the Joyfx Clean Boost delivers clear and powerful signal enhancement.
