MOOG MF-104M Analog Delay





The Moog MF-104M Analog Delay is a high-end bucket-brigade delay pedal designed to deliver rich vintage echoes with modern control features. Known for its warm analog repeats and deep modulation capabilities, the MF-104M offers delay times up to one second while maintaining classic analog character. Built-in modulation allows users to add chorus-like movement and tape-style wobble to delay repeats. Extensive control options include tap tempo, MIDI synchronization, and expression pedal support for real-time manipulation. The pedal’s analog signal path ensures organic tonal warmth, while digital control expands creative flexibility. The Moog MF-104M has become a highly respected delay unit among professional guitarists and producers.
