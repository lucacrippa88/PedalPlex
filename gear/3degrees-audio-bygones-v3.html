3Degrees Audio Bygones V3





The 3Degrees Audio Bygones V3 is a unique delay pedal designed to recreate the lo-fi character of vintage digital delay units. Instead of pristine repeats, the Bygones V3 delivers gritty, degraded echoes reminiscent of early rack processors and classic studio effects. Players can shape the tone and texture of the repeats to create everything from subtle ambience to heavily degraded delay trails. The pedal excels at adding character and atmosphere to guitar parts, making it popular among experimental players and producers. Its flexible controls allow users to dial in darker echoes, bit-reduced textures, or glitch-like delay effects. Built with boutique craftsmanship, the Bygones V3 offers a distinctive alternative to traditional delay pedals.
