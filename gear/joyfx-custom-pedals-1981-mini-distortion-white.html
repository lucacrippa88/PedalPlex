Joyfx Custom Pedals 1981 Mini Distortion - White





The Joyfx Custom Pedals 1981 Mini Distortion White edition provides the same responsive boutique-inspired distortion found in the original model, packaged in a striking white enclosure. Designed for modern players who want punchy gain and excellent note clarity, this pedal produces tight low end, focused mids, and a smooth top end that works well for rock, indie, and alternative tones. With straightforward gain, tone, and volume controls, it’s easy to dial in everything from crunchy rhythm sounds to singing lead distortion. The circuit remains highly dynamic and responds well to guitar volume adjustments, allowing expressive control directly from the instrument. Its compact format makes it ideal for smaller pedalboards or minimalist rigs. Built with attention to detail by Joyfx Custom Pedals, the 1981 Mini Distortion White edition combines boutique-style tone, responsive gain structure, and a clean visual aesthetic.
