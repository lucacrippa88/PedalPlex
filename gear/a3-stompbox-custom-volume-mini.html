A3 Stompbox Custom Volume (Mini)





The A3 Stompbox Custom Volume (Mini) is a compact volume pedal designed to provide smooth level control while saving pedalboard space. Despite its smaller footprint, the pedal delivers precise volume adjustments suitable for swells, fade-ins, and dynamic playing techniques. Its durable construction ensures reliable performance in both live and studio environments. The mini format makes it ideal for crowded pedalboards or minimalist rigs. Guitarists and bassists can place the pedal before or after gain stages depending on whether they want to control input drive or overall output level.
