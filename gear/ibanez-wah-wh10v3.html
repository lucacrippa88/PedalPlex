Ibanez Wah WH10V3





The Ibanez WH10V3 is the modern reissue of the famous wah pedal associated with John Frusciante. This third version retains the distinctive wide sweep and vocal character of the original while improving durability and output consistency. It features dedicated modes for both guitar and bass, allowing players to adapt the wah response to different instruments and frequency ranges. The adjustable depth control lets users fine-tune the intensity of the wah effect, making it possible to achieve subtle tonal movement or dramatic filtering. Known for its expressive feel and musical sweep, the WH10V3 continues the legacy of one of the most recognizable wah pedals in alternative and funk rock tones.
