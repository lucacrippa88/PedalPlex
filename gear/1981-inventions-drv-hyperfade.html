Description

The 1981 Inventions DRV (Hyperfade) combines boutique build quality with a carefully refined distortion circuit inspired by the legendary RAT. Known for its articulate response and balanced EQ, the DRV delivers a versatile gain range suitable for everything from edge-of-breakup drive to saturated distortion. The pedal features a straightforward three-control layout—Volume, Tone, and Drive—making it easy to sculpt tones that remain clear and focused in a mix. Unlike many high-gain pedals, the DRV maintains note separation and dynamic feel, responding well to picking attack and guitar volume changes. The Hyperfade finish gives this edition a distinctive visual identity while preserving the same highly regarded sonic performance. Guitarists often use the DRV as a primary distortion or stack it with boosts and overdrives for expanded tonal flexibility.
