3Leaf Audio Doom 2 Fuzz





The 3Leaf Audio Doom 2 is a modern fuzz pedal designed to deliver powerful low-end response and aggressive saturation. Originally developed with bass players in mind, the Doom 2 also performs exceptionally well with guitar, producing thick fuzz tones that remain articulate and punchy. The pedal blends fuzz with a clean signal path, preserving clarity while adding massive harmonic texture. This makes it ideal for players who want heavy fuzz tones without losing definition in the mix. Known for its dynamic feel and strong output, the Doom 2 can easily drive amplifiers and other pedals.
