Behringer Heavy Distortion HD300





The Behringer HD300 Heavy Distortion is designed to deliver aggressive high-gain tones suitable for modern rock and metal guitar styles. Distortion pedals amplify and clip the guitar signal to produce powerful saturation, tight low end, and enhanced sustain. The HD300 provides a wide gain range that allows players to move from crunchy rhythm tones to intense lead distortion. Its tone controls help shape the overall character of the distortion, making it adaptable to different guitars and amplifiers. Discover high-gain distortion tones with the Behringer HD300 on PedalPlex.
