Joyfx Custom Pedals Fuzz War





The Joyfx Custom Pedals Fuzz War is a powerful fuzz pedal designed to deliver aggressive, saturated tones with massive sustain and thick harmonic character. Inspired by classic high-gain fuzz circuits, this pedal produces a wide range of textures from gritty vintage fuzz to heavy, modern saturation. The circuit emphasizes strong low-end response and cutting mids, ensuring that the fuzz tone remains present in dense band mixes. Controls allow players to shape gain intensity, tone balance, and output level, making the pedal adaptable for different guitars and amplifiers. When paired with overdrives or boosts, the Fuzz War can produce even more extreme textures and layered distortion sounds. Built with Joyfx Custom Pedals’ commitment to quality craftsmanship, this fuzz pedal is ideal for players seeking bold, expressive fuzz tones for rock, alternative, and experimental music.
