JHS Rotary Chorus 3 series





The JHS 3 Series Rotary Chorus blends traditional chorus modulation with rotary speaker-style movement. The effect recreates the swirling character of vintage rotating speaker cabinets while maintaining the lush modulation of a chorus pedal. Players can adjust the speed and intensity of the modulation to achieve subtle shimmer or dramatic rotating textures. This makes the pedal suitable for classic rock, psychedelic music, and ambient styles. The Rotary Chorus offers a unique hybrid modulation effect in a compact and easy-to-use format.
