Description

The 1981 Inventions DRV (Hyperfade White) is a special finish version of the acclaimed DRV distortion pedal, known for its articulate gain and refined take on the classic RAT-style circuit. Delivering everything from gritty drive to full-bodied distortion, the DRV is designed to retain clarity and punch even at higher gain settings. The three-knob interface—Volume, Tone, and Drive—offers precise control over output level, brightness, and saturation. Players value the DRV for its tight low end, smooth clipping character, and the way it preserves the character of the guitar and amplifier. Whether used as a main distortion or stacked with boosts and overdrives, it maintains excellent note definition and dynamic response.
