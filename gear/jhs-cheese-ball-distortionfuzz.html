JHS Cheese Ball Distortion/Fuzz





The JHS Cheese Ball is a vintage-inspired fuzz and distortion pedal modeled after rare British fuzz circuits from the late 1960s and early 1970s. It delivers thick, saturated fuzz tones with a distinctive midrange character and a slightly gated texture when pushed harder. The pedal includes multiple clipping modes that allow players to move between smooth fuzz sustain and more aggressive distortion-style tones. This flexibility makes it suitable for garage rock, psychedelic music, and modern alternative styles. With its unique voice and dynamic response, the Cheese Ball captures the quirky charm of vintage fuzz designs while remaining highly usable in contemporary pedalboard setups.
