Keeley Black Glass British Fuzz - Gold





The Keeley Black Glass British Fuzz Gold edition captures the sound of vintage British fuzz circuits known for their gritty texture and powerful sustain. Inspired by classic fuzz tones from the golden era of rock, this pedal delivers thick distortion with strong mids and a cutting top end. The circuit responds dynamically to picking attack and guitar volume adjustments, allowing players to shape everything from raw vintage fuzz to more controlled overdrive textures. The Gold edition features a distinctive finish that highlights its boutique design. With its rich harmonic content and expressive feel, the Black Glass British Fuzz is ideal for players seeking classic fuzz tones with modern reliability.
