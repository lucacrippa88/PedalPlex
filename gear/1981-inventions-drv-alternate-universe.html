1981 Inventions DRV Alternate Universe





The 1981 Inventions DRV Alternate Universe edition delivers the same acclaimed distortion circuit that made the DRV a modern boutique favorite. Inspired by the classic RAT design, the pedal offers a versatile gain range that moves from gritty overdrive to powerful distortion while maintaining excellent note clarity. With its simple control layout—Volume, Tone, and Drive—players can quickly shape tones suited for rhythm, lead, or stacked gain setups. The DRV is praised for its tight low end, balanced mids, and smooth top end, allowing it to cut through a mix without harshness. Its dynamic response makes it sensitive to picking attack and guitar volume adjustments. The Alternate Universe finish adds a unique collectible look while retaining the pedal’s refined sonic performance.
