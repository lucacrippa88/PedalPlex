MOOG MF-103 12 Stage Phaser





The Moog MF-103 12 Stage Phaser is a premium analog phaser pedal known for its lush modulation and extensive control options. Unlike typical phasers, the MF-103 offers selectable 6-stage and 12-stage phasing, allowing players to choose between subtle movement and deep swirling textures. Built-in LFO modulation provides adjustable rate and depth, while resonance and sweep controls enable dramatic filter sweeps. The pedal also supports external control via expression pedals and control voltage inputs, making it highly versatile for experimental setups. With its warm analog circuitry and Moog ladder filter design elements, the MF-103 produces rich and musical phasing effects ideal for guitar, bass, and synthesizers.
