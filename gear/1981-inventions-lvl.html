1981 Inventions LVL





The 1981 Inventions LVL is a high-headroom boost pedal designed to enhance clarity, dynamics, and overall tone. Built with the same boutique attention to detail as the DRV, the LVL delivers clean gain that can push an amplifier into natural breakup or elevate existing drive pedals. The pedal provides exceptional transparency, allowing the original character of the guitar and amp to remain intact while adding presence and punch. With its straightforward control layout and premium components, the LVL works equally well as a solo boost, always-on tone enhancer, or signal driver for complex pedalboard setups. Its high headroom design ensures clean operation even with powerful pickups and stacked effects. Guitarists looking for a refined and musical boost will appreciate how the LVL improves articulation and responsiveness.
