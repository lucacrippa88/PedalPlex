Klon Centaur Original - Grey





The Klon Centaur Original Grey is an early and extremely collectible version of the legendary Klon overdrive pedal. Celebrated for its transparent tone and exceptional dynamic response, the Centaur enhances an amplifier’s natural character while adding harmonic richness and sustain. Its unique circuit blends a clean signal with an overdriven signal, allowing notes to remain clear and articulate even at higher gain settings. The pedal excels as both a subtle overdrive and a powerful clean boost for driving tube amplifiers. The simple control layout—Gain, Treble, and Output—offers a wide range of tones suitable for blues, rock, and modern guitar styles. The rare grey enclosure version is especially valued by collectors and tone purists. The Klon Centaur Original Grey continues to represent one of the most influential boutique overdrive designs in the history of guitar effects.
