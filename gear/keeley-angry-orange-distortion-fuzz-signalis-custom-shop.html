Keeley Angry Orange Distortion & Fuzz - Signalis Custom Shop





The Keeley Angry Orange Distortion & Fuzz Signalis Custom Shop edition delivers the same powerful distortion and fuzz circuitry as the standard model while featuring a limited custom shop aesthetic. Designed to capture the aggressive energy of classic British drive tones combined with thick vintage fuzz textures, the pedal offers a broad range of gain levels suitable for modern rock and alternative guitar styles. The circuit maintains strong note clarity even when heavily saturated, ensuring chords remain articulate and lead lines sustain smoothly. Players can shape the tone using intuitive gain, tone, and level controls, adapting the pedal to different guitars and amplifier setups. As part of Keeley’s Custom Shop lineup, this version reflects the company’s commitment to craftsmanship, tone, and collectible design.
