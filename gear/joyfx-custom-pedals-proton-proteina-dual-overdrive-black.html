Joyfx Custom Pedals Proton / Proteina Dual Overdrive - Black





The Joyfx Custom Pedals Proton Proteina Dual Overdrive Black edition provides the same flexible two-channel overdrive platform as the original model, presented in a sleek black enclosure. Each side of the pedal offers independent gain and tone shaping, allowing players to craft different drive textures or stack both circuits for richer saturation. One channel focuses on transparent low-gain drive while the other provides thicker overdrive suitable for lead tones. This combination makes the pedal highly adaptable for both studio recording and live performance. With its responsive dynamics and clear note definition, the Proton Proteina Dual Overdrive Black edition offers versatile drive tones in a professional pedalboard format.
