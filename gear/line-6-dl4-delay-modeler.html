Line 6 DL4 Delay Modeler





The Line 6 DL4 Delay Modeler is one of the most influential delay pedals ever produced. Known for its wide range of delay algorithms and intuitive stompbox interface, the DL4 recreates classic analog, tape, and digital delay sounds along with unique creative effects. The pedal provides multiple presets that can be accessed instantly during performance, making it a favorite among touring musicians. Additional features include a built-in looper, tap tempo, and extended delay times suitable for ambient soundscapes. The DL4’s warm delay tones and flexible controls allow guitarists to create everything from subtle slapback echoes to expansive atmospheric textures. Its rugged green enclosure and user-friendly layout have made it a staple on professional pedalboards for decades.
