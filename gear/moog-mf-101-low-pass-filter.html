MOOG MF-101 Low Pass Filter





The Moog MF-101 Low Pass Filter is a classic analog filter pedal derived from Moog’s legendary synthesizer technology. Designed to bring the iconic Moog ladder filter sound to guitarists and bassists, the MF-101 provides rich, warm filtering with powerful resonance and envelope control. The pedal can create smooth tone shaping, funky auto-wah effects, and deep synth-like textures. Controls for cutoff frequency, resonance, envelope amount, and mix allow extensive tonal experimentation. The MF-101 also supports expression pedal control for real-time parameter modulation. Built with high-quality analog components, the pedal delivers the unmistakable sonic character that made Moog filters famous in electronic music and beyond.
